Samantha Spafford

Samantha Spafford

Hi and thanks for checking out my profile! Its really important to choose the practitioner and the approach that is a 'good fit' with your personality and goals. My methods are incredibly practical, in our sessions you can expect for us to be discussing your goals, measuring progress and together overcoming any hurdles that come up along the way. Some people have a very clear idea about what they would like to work on, while others have a vague sense that things could be better without knowing exactly where to start, both of which are absolutely fine. Clarifying what is important to you and which direction you would like to be moving is often helpful in itself.

Carol Schultz

Is making positive change in your life a daily struggle? I help everyday people who struggle with day-to-day life to make positive changes. You may need a one-off session for some helpful guidance on something small or you may want long term help to come to terms with a difficult situation. Either way, where I can help, I always do.
I focus our counseling services on offering help to people in a way that meets their personal needs. I make sure that every person I help can access online counseling in a way that makes them feel comfortable, empowered and supported.
